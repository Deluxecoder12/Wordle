How to Play Wordle Unlimited?

Welcome to Wordle Unlimited! Here's how you can enjoy the game:

Objective

Your goal is to guess the secret 5-letter word within six attempts. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.

How to Play?

Enter Your Guess:

  1. Type any valid 5-letter word in the input box and press Enter or click the Submit button.
  2. The game will compare your guess to the secret word and provide feedback.

Feedback:

Correct letter and position

Correct letter but wrong position

Letter not in the word

Examples

Here are some examples to help you understand how feedback is given:

Example 1:

Guess: CRANE

Feedback:

C R A N E

Explanation: The letter C is in the correct position, R is in the word but not in the correct position, and the other letters are not in the word.

Example 2:

Guess: STARE

Feedback:

S T A R E

Explanation: The letter A is in the correct position, and the other letters are not in the word.

Strategy

Use the feedback to refine your guesses. For example, if a letter is green, try to keep it in the same position in your next guesses. If it's yellow, move it to another position.

Win or Lose

If you guess the word correctly within six tries, you win!

If you don't guess the word in six tries, the game will reveal the secret word.

Unlimited Play

There's no limit to how many times you can play! Refresh the page or click the Wordle Unlimited button to start a new game with a new word.

Tips

  1. Start with a word that includes common vowels and consonants, like "audio" or "crane."
  2. Think carefully about each letter's position based on the feedback you receive.
  3. Use each guess strategically to eliminate as many letters as possible.

Good luck, and enjoy playing Wordle Unlimited!
